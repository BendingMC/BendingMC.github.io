BendingMC Features

Along with the key aspect of bending all four elements, BendingMC allows players use their abilities for different playstyles.

Bending Arena

Experience the main spotlight of bending. Compete in duels or all out mayhem in bending themed arenas.

Towny

Towny is the protection plugin for your towns and cities, nations will rise and fall under your command. Lead us to glory or death!

Mini Games

With special events, we mix mini games and bending together. Giving players another way to play and experience BendingMC.

Plot World

Experience the world of bending. Create and build a massive structure. Or look and see what other players have made. 

Best Minecraft Benders

Make your way to the top of the leaderboard. Verse and compete against other players for a place on the hall of benders.

Social Media

Join us on social media for up to date posts about BendingMC. Be one of the first people to get information on events, updates, and sales.
